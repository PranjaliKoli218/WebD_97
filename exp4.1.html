DYPCET
D.Y. Patil College of Engineering & Technology (DYPCET), Kolhapur, established in 1984, is a premier autonomous, NAAC 'A' grade accredited engineering institution affiliated with Shivaji University. It offers undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including CSE, Mechanical, and Civil, with a focus on industry-ready skills and strong placement record
DYPSEM
D.Y. Patil School of Engineering & Management (DYPSEM) in Kolhapur, established in 2023 and approved by AICTE, is the 8th constituent unit of D.Y. Patil Education Society (Deemed to be University). It offers B.Tech, MBA, and MCA programs, focusing on emerging technologies like AI/ML, Data Science, and Computer Engineering.
KIT
KIT's College of Engineering, Kolhapur (KITCoEK), established in 1983, is a premier, autonomous, and NAAC A+ accredited institution in Maharashtra offering UG, PG, and PhD programs in engineering. Known for strong placements in Computer Science and related fields, it features a 30-acre campus with extensive infrastructure.

RIT
Rajarambapu Institute of Technology (RIT), located in Rajaramnagar, Maharashtra, is a premier autonomous engineering institute established in 1983. It offers UG and PG programs in engineering (including AICTE-approved, NBA-accredited courses), MBA, and boasts a 43-hectare green campus, with a focus on outcome-based education, research, and high-quality placements.
